Since 1951:

AITP has been a continuing resource for quality professional development. Serving the IT industry as a national organization and through our local chapters, AITP is committed to delivering to our members the recognition, support, and credibility that will give them the competitive edge needed to succeed. AITP seeks to advance the support of IT education and national policies on IT that improve society as a whole.

By Joining AITP:

You become part of a strong network of IT professionals, have access to empowering resources and opportunities, and are better positioned to achieve your professional goals. The organization seeks to provide avenues for all members to be teachers as well as students and to make contacts with other members in the IT field - in an effort to become more marketable in rapidly changing technological careers.

AITP Members:

Span every level of the IT industry - from mainframe systems to micro systems; from PC based LAN and WAN systems to virtual systems and the internet - AITP has special niches that cater to the special interests of our members. Our members are found in every facet of society: colleges and universities, banking, industry, retail, the armed forces, government, hospitals, and beyond.
